Note for the CFO Function
Wire transfer, prepaid monthly. No deliverable-gated invoicing. No floating receivables.

Family offices retain outside counsel, accountants, and senior operating advisors on prepaid wire transfers as standard practice. The mobilization payment borrows construction-subcontractor language and operates on the same logic. Cash flow stays clean on both sides; neither party is ever owed work, and neither is ever in float. Production work is fifty-fifty on each SOW with seven-day wire terms — same architecture Rob already uses with construction subs.

04 · Trigger Metrics

Seven measurable checks at Day 90. Six of seven trigger the rest of the year.

The trigger metrics are designed to be binary, auditable, and bounded. No interpretive scoring. No subjective evaluations. Either the work shipped or it didn't. Six of seven landing triggers automatic continuation of months 4 through 12 at the same terms.

What happens after 90 days?
If the pilot hits — 6 of 7 triggers
Months 4–12 continue automatically, at the same terms. No renegotiation. The engagement becomes the full twelve-month commitment.
If the pilot misses — 5 or fewer
Clean exit at Day 90. AWC keeps the work product, VDO Aigency keeps the mobilization. No obligation beyond the 90 days.
The risk is bounded to 90 days. The upside is a full year of a proven function. AWC only commits to the twelve months if the pilot has already earned it.

06 · Why This Structure Works

Bounded, measured, triggerable. Designed for the CFO function.

Bounded commitment.

$54,400 total exposure for 90 days. No multi-year handshake. AWC commits to a pilot, not a contract.

Wire-prepaid, no Net 30.

Same payment architecture AWC uses for outside counsel and senior subs. No floating receivables, no chasing invoices, no float game.

Real trigger mechanism.

Six of seven binary metrics at Day 90. The metrics are measurable, the trigger is automatic, and the off-ramp is clean.

Lane discipline with BBG.

BBG handles brand books and static identity work. VDO Aigency handles moving content, executive voice, and operating cadence. No overlap.

The proving ground exists.

Leopardo Efficiency has been operating this model for months. The pilot is replication of a proven system, not invention of a new one.

One executive voice per company.

The Curtis Leopardo LinkedIn presence proved the model: prospects research leadership before they take a meeting. The pilot builds one groomed thought-leadership presence per portfolio company — the same play already run for Jim Leopardo, extended across the portfolio.

Clean exit at Day 90.

If the pilot misses its metrics, AWC keeps the work product, VDO Aigency keeps the mobilization, and the engagement ends without lawyers or drama.
