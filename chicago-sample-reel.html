Leopardo Efficiency

Heather Garay

Partnership Executive

"Before I joined, I was the finance director for a municipality — I was the client this program was built for."

Heather sat on the other side of the table before she joined the team, and talks candidly about what actually won her over: a Leopardo rep showing up with organized spreadsheets and following through. It's the credibility story told from the buyer's seat.

Grand Kahn Electric

Mike Casto

President

"We're not the biggest electrical contractor in Chicago. We've just been here the longest."

119 years, Chicago's first registered electrical contractor, and a client list running from Tesla's Melrose Park training facility to Rush Cancer Center — delivered with the deadpan confidence of a company that doesn't need to oversell it.

Laystrom Hardware

Chuck Thomas

Executive

"We've gone from a traditional old-school hardware manufacturer to a technically savvy distributor."

Chuck lays out the range in plain terms — doors, frames, and hardware for commercial builds coast to coast — and the real differentiator: modern software behind old-school product knowledge, since the Laystrom acquisition in 2023.

Leopardo Construction

Sean Scott

Senior Project Manager

"It's family owned. It's got that family feel — and I love being here."

Filmed on-site at The Ave, a four-story, 52-unit mixed-use build in progress. Sean walks the property while talking about the job, which is exactly the kind of on-location, in-motion footage the recruiting and owner-facing content needs more of.

Leopardo Efficiency

Drake Wahlers

Vice President, Engineering — Leopardo Energy

"I felt like I was working for a startup company, but with the backing and credibility of a large construction firm."

Drake joined in 2015 after leaving a large contractor looking for more movement — and lays out the Cleveland, South Euclid, and Speedway, Indiana wins as proof the trust with municipalities was earned, not assumed.
