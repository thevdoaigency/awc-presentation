02 · State of the Brand Today

A 117-year-old contractor with a website frozen in 2022.

Page 02

Grand Kahn Electric is one of the most credentialed electrical contractors in Chicago. The current website does not reflect that.

The site reads as if it was completed in mid-2022 and never touched again. The most recent news post is from November 2022. The EV charging vertical — explicitly named on the site as “the future” — has a dedicated landing page that has not been updated in three years. A buyer landing here in 2026 sees a company that has stopped speaking.

What works.

Project Portfolio
A deep, organized project library spanning healthcare, hospitality, education, transportation, and corporate office work. Every photo is publishable. Every brand name is recognizable.
Service Architecture
Clear vertical structure: electrical and field engineering, across nine industries. The taxonomy is correct.
Visual Foundation
Existing template and project tile structure is functional. A redesign does not require rebuilding from zero.

What does not.

No Story
117 years of Chicago history is not mentioned anywhere prominent. No founding story. No generational lineage. No reason for a buyer to trust this firm over a younger competitor.
Stale Signal
News page dead since November 2022. EV charging page untouched since July 2022. A live business should not look dormant.
No Voice
No founder presence. No executive thought leadership. No social channels actively connected to the brand. The company speaks only through project photos.
03 · Historical Wins

The project portfolio is the best argument the brand never makes.

Page 03

A look at three projects that, told properly, would carry Grand Kahn’s positioning for the next decade.

Transit Infrastructure

Ogilvie Transportation Center

One of Chicago’s two main commuter rail terminals. Hundreds of thousands of riders move through it every week. Grand Kahn handled the electrical infrastructure. Almost no one outside the trade knows this.

Iconic American Brand

Radio Flyer Headquarters

Chicago’s most beloved heritage brand. The little red wagon every American grew up with. Grand Kahn wired their headquarters facility. The most narratively powerful project in the portfolio — and the least promoted.

Hospitality & Healthcare Depth

Hyatt · Loews · NCH · DuPage Medical

Hyatt Regency, Loews Chicago, NCH Outpatient Care, Central DuPage Hospital, Duly Westmont, DuPage Medical Group Lockport. Three hospitality flagships and four medical office buildings. No competitor in the Chicago market has this combined depth.

Additional notable projects buried in the portfolio: Columbia College Chicago, Capgemini, Zebra Technologies, Fragomen, Merisant, Scotsman / ALI Group, Camping World, Lexus of Naperville, Park Pointe Memory Care, FBRK Impact House, Y.O.U. Headquarters & Educational Center.
04 · The Brand Gap

What it costs to not tell the story.

Page 04

A company that built Chicago’s infrastructure for 117 years is competing for jobs on price.

Without a clear brand narrative, Grand Kahn is comparable to any electrical contractor with a portfolio page. The 117 years of lineage, the Ogilvie credential, the Radio Flyer story, the hospital and hospitality depth — none of it shows up in the buyer’s decision. Procurement leaders, developers, and general contractors choose on the variables they can see. Right now those variables are bid price and proximity.

What buyers can’t see
Four generations of family business. Chicago infrastructure built. Hospital and hospitality track records the competition cannot match.
What buyers see instead
A project gallery from 2022. A news page that stopped publishing. An EV charging page that promises the future but hasn’t been updated since.
What the competition does
Newer contractors with smaller portfolios are running active LinkedIn presences, publishing project completions weekly, and showing up first in Chicago commercial RFP searches.
The compounding cost
Every quarter Grand Kahn doesn’t tell its story, a newer competitor builds the brand Grand Kahn already earned. Brand equity is finite, and silence transfers it.
05 · Recommendations

Five moves over the first 90 days.

Page 05

Modest in cost. Significant in compound effect. None require rebuilding the site from zero.

01

Activate the 117-year story.

Add a founding narrative to the About page. Bring the family lineage forward. Position the EV vertical as the next generation continuing the work. A 117-year-old company that doesn’t talk about its history is invisible to the half of the buying audience that values it most.

02

Publish three case study films.

Ogilvie. Radio Flyer. NCH Outpatient. Each one a 60–90 second cinematic story from completed work. Distributed across the website, LinkedIn, and direct outreach to developers and general contractors. Real footage, real pride, real proof.

03

Restart the news cadence.

One project completion post per month. Two thought-leadership posts per quarter from leadership. Modest cadence, sustained for twelve months, restores the “active company” signal that a stale news page actively undermines.

04

Rebuild the EV vertical page.

EV charging is named as a strategic growth bet. The page representing that bet was last updated in 2022. Refresh the case studies, clarify the buyer (commercial developers, fleet operators, multi-family residential), and quote a real Grand Kahn engineer talking about why this is the next 117 years of Chicago electrical.

05

Introduce a face to the brand.

Procurement directors and developers buy from people they recognize. A senior member of Grand Kahn becomes the public voice — at least two LinkedIn posts a week, an occasional podcast appearance, a regular byline on the company news page. Trade work is built on relationships. The brand should reflect that.

06

Adopt the AWC portfolio identity system.

Grand Kahn currently exists in its own visual silo. Aligning with the broader Alexander West Capital design language — shared typography, shared color discipline, shared content infrastructure — produces immediate cohesion across portfolio touchpoints without erasing what makes Grand Kahn distinct.

06 · Proposed Positioning
Chicago’s first electrical contractor.
Building the next 117 years.
A line that names the lineage and the future in one breath. Works on a website, in an RFP cover, on a job site banner, and at the top of every piece of marketing material the company produces.
